Q - 1. What are the advantages of Bootstrap?
Ans.

Following are the advantages of Bootstrap :

1. Responsive Design: Easily create websites that are responsive.
2. Consistency: Ensures a uniform look and feel across the site.
3. Customizable: We can customize styles according to project needs.
4. Cross-browser Compatibility: Works well across different browsers.
5. Time-saving: Speeds up development with pre-made components.
6. Integration: Seamless integration with JavaScript libraries.
7. Accessibility: Built-in features for improved accessibility.
8. Scalability: Suits projects of all sizes, from simple to complex.
